### Waterfall charts

Waterfall charts show a running total as AnswerRocket adds and subtracts values. They're useful for understanding how an initial value (like net income) is affected by a series of positive and negative changes.

The columns are color coded so you can quickly notice increases and decreases. The initial and the final value columns often start on the horizontal axis, while the intermediate values are floating columns.
